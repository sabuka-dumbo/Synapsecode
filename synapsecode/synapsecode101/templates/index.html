Who we are

Synapse Code is Georgia’s first non-profit at the intersection of neuroscience and programming, empowering young innovators worldwide through mentorship, collaboration, and critical thinking. Our global team of mentors from Canada, Georgia, Nepal, and beyond works to inspire curiosity and prepare the next generation to connect human and technological minds.
